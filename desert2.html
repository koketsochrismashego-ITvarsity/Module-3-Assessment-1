Malva pudding with Caramel Sauce

Prep time : 1 hour 30min

Yield: 8-10

Ingredients















Steps

  1. Place the milk, butter and jam in a saucepan on medium heat. Stir over until the butter abd jam have melted. Add vinegar to the mixture then set aside. (At this point it looks like Inkomazi)
  2. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs & beat until light and fluffy
  3. Sift flour, BP, bicarbonate,salt and add to the egg mixture, at same time with the milk mixture.
  4. Pour into a large greased dish and bake in a preheated oven at 180c for 35-45 minutes.
  5. SAUCE: combine the milk, sugar, and butter in a small saucepan. Simmer for about 3 minutes.
  6. Remove from tne heat and add the essence.
  7. Remove pudding from oven, prick the top and pour sauce over
  8. Serve with custard/ice cream or Cream
