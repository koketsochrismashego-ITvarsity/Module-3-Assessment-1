Creamy Peri chicken livers

Prep time : 30 - 45 min

Ingredients



Steps

  1. Add 1/3 of cooking oil in a pan
  2. Put the onions in
  3. When your onions are cooked well, add your livers
  4. Leave the livers for a minute on one side before you turn them over to avoid them breaking down
  5. Add garlic
  6. If you do not like spicy food, you can substitute this with Cayenne or Paprika
  7. Sprinkle of thyme, Black pepper, 1 cube of knorox chicken flavour
  8. Cook and stir until they done on both sides
  9. Add the Peri Peri sauce
  10. Toss and add fresh cream, about half a cup
  11. Stir and let it simmer for just 2 minutes
  12. Dish out, Final product could be served with Toasted bruschetta or garlic bread
