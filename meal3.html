Ox Liver

Prep time : 30 - 45 min

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Remove the outer part of the liver thag looks like plastic
  2. Add aromat on the Liver
  3. Bit of baking powder to soften the liver
  4. Let is sit in the refrigerator for an hour or so
  5. Over Medium Heat, add oil in Pan
  6. Fry your liver for a minute on each side.
  7. Remove your liver, and in same pan Fry your onion rings until they turn Golden brown,
  8. Add your back, add your spices and herbs, tomato paste
  9. Add 1 and half cup water, salt, pepper and cover.
  10. The tomato paste helps to thicken your sauce, in 20 minutes your liver should be done.
  11. Do not overcook your liver as this will turn it to rubber texture
